AUTOBIOGRAPHY

You might wonder who is behind those photos. If that's the case, you are in the right place to find out. I was born in France of both French parents. Since I was a very young child, I had the chance to traveled a lot. Later, I will be push into my dad's passion, the photography. He used to show me some of them and I've just find it amazing. Afterwards, he brought me to several photography museums and taught me how to use photoshop. I've then developing a strong desire for photography and decided to take photo on my own.
