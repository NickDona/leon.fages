STREET PHOTOGRAPHY

This style of photography was an inspiration to me from living around these people here in China.
Through this style I like to take photos when everything around me is moving. I enjoy to capturing people for who they are, not when posing,
just living their lives. As a recent resident of China I wanted to show, in an aesthetic way, the daily life of Chinese folk.
